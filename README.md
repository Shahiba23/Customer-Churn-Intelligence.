# 📌 Project Summary

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer data to understand why customers leave, identify high-risk customer segments, and estimate the financial impact of churn.

The complete analytics pipeline was built using SQL for data extraction and Python for data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), KPI generation, and business insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The objective of this project is to:

- Identify customers likely to churn
- Understand the major factors contributing to churn
- Measure revenue loss due to customer churn
- Compare churn across different subscription plans
- Recommend strategies to improve customer retention

# 📈 Key Findings

- Overall customer churn rate: **28.6%**
- Monthly subscription customers showed significantly higher churn than annual subscribers.
- Monthly plan churn: **55.6%**
- Annual plan churn: **8.3%**
- Estimated revenue loss due to churn: **18%**
- Estimated Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) loss: **₹2,047**
- Built more than **20 business KPIs** for customer performance monitoring.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Encourage customers to switch from monthly to annual subscriptions.
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
- Target high-risk customers with personalized retention campaigns.
- Offer loyalty rewards for long-term customers.
- Monitor churn KPIs regularly using dashboards.
